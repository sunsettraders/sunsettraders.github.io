Brunner's Kona Coffee has been around for about 30 years. What started out as a hobby quickly became a passion for Ed. Our medium roast has proven to be the favorite amongst our friends and family. While drinking it, you will smell the aromas of 100% Kona Coffee along with that sweet taste of Aloha.
